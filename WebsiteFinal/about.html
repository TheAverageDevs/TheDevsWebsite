About Luko Studios

Where Are You Located?

Luko Studios is located in the nature filled country of New Zealand

What Game Engine Do You Use?

Luko Studios uses the game engine of Roblox Studio, Roblox Studio is a free to use studio which gets developers to develop their games from scratch. The image below shows an example of the base template which Roblox Studio gives developers to develop and create their games on.

What Games Are You Currently Working On?

Luko Studios is currently working on two games! Disconnected and Underlife, these two games are currently in development and are planned to release sometime in the future. Luko Studios also plans to release more fun packed games in the future from running your own train station to building a castle empire.

Do You Currently Have Any Released Games?

Yes! Currently we have 2 released games. The Cabin, The Cabin is a horror game based in a small cabin in the Canadian forest, The Cabin gets the player to explore the Canadian forest and solve problems before the wicked beast finds them, from puzzle solving to jumpscares The Cabin isn't for the faint hearted. Another game released by Luko Studios is Bunker Survival Tycoon, Bunker Survival Tycoon is an economy based game which gets the player to build their dream bunker after a world ending event. Bunker Survival gets players to earn money by producing products which they can sell on the market to earn money to upgrade their bunker.

What Coding Language Do You Use?

Luko Studios codes in the coding language LUA, LUA is a lightweight, high-level, multi-paradigm programming language designed primarily for embedded use in applications. LUA is used in all of our games and is fit for purpose and allows us to customize our games however we wish. Luko Studios uses a modified version of LUA to ensure Luko Studios games run perfectly on every device.
