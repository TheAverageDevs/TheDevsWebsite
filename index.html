Dunedin History

Welcome to our Dunedin History website. We are a group of three highschool students who want to use our skills to help others learn about the fantastic town of Dunedin.

Dunedin is located in the south island of New Zealand and is known for the student life and the historic gold rush.

Want to help us out with our website? Send your ideas, feedback and information to our email TheAverageDevs@gmail.com and we will be happy to add it to our website.
